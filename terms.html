2. App Description

Wird is a Muslim prayer habit tracker that helps you log your five daily prayers, build streaks, and track your consistency over time. The app is available on iOS through the Apple App Store.

3. Subscriptions & Pricing

Wird offers both free and premium tiers. Premium features are available through a subscription:

4. Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. Your Apple ID account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period at the same price.

5. Free Trial

The yearly plan includes a 3-day free trial. You can use all premium features during the trial period at no cost. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your subscription will automatically begin and your Apple ID account will be charged the yearly subscription price ($39.99 USD).

Free trial eligibility is determined by Apple and is typically available once per Apple ID.

6. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple device settings:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad.
  • Tap your name at the top.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Select Wird and tap Cancel Subscription.

Wird cannot cancel subscriptions on your behalf. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you will retain access to premium features until then. No refunds are provided for partial billing periods.
